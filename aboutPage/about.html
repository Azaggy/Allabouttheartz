About the Artist

All About The Artz (A.K.A. Anamaria Zaggy) has been doing artwork since she was in middle school. She is based in the Saint Louis Area in Missouri. She does not specialize in one specific medium, but uses a good majority of them in her art. She is always looking to improve her work and broaden her spectrum of knowledge.

At the age of 16, All About The Artz found herself helping on a mural called "Reflective Mythologies: Portraits (re-mixed)". This mural explored mythmaking through art. It was lead by artist, Christopher Burch.

All About The Artz chose to touch on her Mayan roots. She picked three photos of herself to protray her personality and who she is as a person. She loves art because to her she can express herseld and it helps her to not care how others view her.

Credit Chelsea Embree | Beacon Intern | 2013

“It means a lot because my heritage is on a wall. I don’t know for how long, but it’s on a wall,” Zaggy said. “The people who see it might not know it, but I know it, the people who I worked with know it, and that’s all that really matters.”
